Every year, many video games are released, quite enough time to play them all. How is one to work out which games are worthwhile? Sites dedicated to the news and reviews of video games are an excellent method to stay informed about the gaming industry and determine whether the newest releases are worthwhile.

Journalism about video games has faced many challenges over the previous ten years. we cannot go into specifics, but suffice it to mention that some well-known video gaming websites have engaged in unethical practices like cronyism, collusion, and secret financial links. additionally , the stress on reviewing games based primarily on their gameplay and quality has changed for many gaming websites. Currently, some websites place a premium on social and political concerns, or maybe have authors who treat some pieces as posts on their personal blogs. To the detriment of their coverage of gaming, several gaming websites have expanded to incorporate coverage of other forms of entertainment (such as movies, TV, and music). We urge you to find out more about these concerns before deciding if you want to stick with such well-known gaming websites. Whatever websites you opt to read, it is important that you focus on the writers, not just the web site . Since the standard of the authors at various sites varies, there's not really a "best game review site" for everyone.
